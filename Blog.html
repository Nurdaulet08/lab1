Second post

15.08.2015

It has been 20 years since I started a new life. During these debts twenty years, a lot has changed, so much that it was possible to write a novel or make a film. I have already forgotten this blog that I thought I would keep. But still I have something to tell. After I left to live in another country. I didn’t even know that I would get to know my future wife Valentona Viktorovna. We got to know her by chance and quickly got to talking, at that time I did not know that she was the daughter of one of the richest man in the world, but not yet about that ... Upon arrival, I do not have many savings and a few acquaintances who could settle in and find a place to live. So in my head I had no more idea of what I would do in this new country ... It's been 2 months since I started living a new life. I found a job where I get not bad money, but in truth I don’t like it. But it’s not long as I save up money, I’ll immediately quit and open my business. I want to open my store. and live a calm life. Without any worries and complete harmony. 5 years have passed I was able to save enough money to open my own business, but one of my friends needs an operation. Of course he did not ask me for help, but I want to help him, but my dream may not come true after that if I give all my money. After all, I myself am deep in debt, but still I want to help him.
